1 foot candle = 1 lumen/sq. foot = 10.76 lux (SI unit for illuminance).

If R(eflectance) = 1.0, then 1 foot candle = 1 foot-lambert = 3.426 cd/m² (SI unit for luminance).

T.Good (pronounced “tee-dot-good”) — the half-stop split between T2.8 and T4. East Coast convention for a default/starting-point lens aperture, such as when the DP has not yet called out a stop.
